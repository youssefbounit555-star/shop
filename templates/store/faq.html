Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

Products & Ordering

Placing an order is simple! Browse our store, select the products you want, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide your shipping address and payment information. Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive an email with your order details and tracking information.

We accept all major payment methods including credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, PayPal, and digital wallets. All payments are processed securely using the latest encryption technology to protect your financial information.

Yes! We offer complimentary gift wrapping for select items. During checkout, you can choose the gift wrapping option and even add a personalized gift message. This is perfect for birthdays, holidays, and special occasions.

Absolutely! Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to monitor your package's journey in real-time. We also provide regular updates through your account dashboard.

Shipping & Delivery

Shipping costs vary based on your location and the weight of your order. We offer free shipping on orders over $50 in most regions. Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days, while express shipping is available for faster delivery.

Yes! We ship to over 150 countries worldwide. International shipping times vary depending on your location but typically take 10-21 business days. Customs duties and taxes may apply to international orders, which are the responsibility of the customer.

We aim to process and ship orders within 1-2 business days. Orders are shipped via trusted carriers. Once shipped, you'll receive a tracking number. We're not responsible for packages lost after they leave our facility, but we'll work with the carrier to investigate any issues.

You can change your delivery address during checkout before placing your order. Once an order has been placed and processed, address changes may not be possible. Contact our customer service immediately if you need to modify your address.

We take great care in packaging all items. If you receive a damaged product, please contact us within 48 hours with photos of the damage. We'll either send a replacement or issue a full refund at no cost to you.

Returns & Warranty

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee! If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days of receipt for a full refund or exchange. Items must be in original condition with all packaging and accessories.

Visit your account on our website, locate the order you wish to return, and click "Return Item". Follow the instructions to generate a return shipping label. Once we receive your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Most of our electronic products come with a 1-year manufacturer's warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Warranty details are included with your product documentation. Extended warranties are available for certain items at checkout.

For defective products or our error, we cover return shipping costs. For regular returns due to change of mind, the customer is responsible for return shipping. However, we provide a prepaid return label for your convenience on most orders.

Yes! You can exchange any product for a different size, color, or item within 30 days. Exchanges are processed quickly, and if the new item is more expensive, you'll only pay the difference. If it's less expensive, we'll refund the difference.

Account & Loyalty

Click on the "Create Account" button in the header, fill in your email address and create a password. You can also sign up with your Google or Facebook account for faster registration. Once confirmed, you'll have access to order history, wishlists, and exclusive deals.

Yes! You can checkout as a guest without creating an account. However, we recommend creating an account so you can track orders, save favorite items, and receive exclusive discounts and early access to sales.

Yes! ElegantShop Plus is our exclusive loyalty program. Members earn points on every purchase that can be redeemed for discounts. You'll also get early access to sales, exclusive products, and special birthday rewards. Join for free when you create an account!

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link via email. Click the link, follow the instructions, and create a new password. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder.
